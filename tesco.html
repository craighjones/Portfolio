Tesco

Conceptualise how digital channels make online grocery shopping a service for better living, for both existing customers and attracting new customers, through freshness, quality and service innovation.

Details

Plan: Inspiration can come at anytime, we wanted to make it super easy taking the chore out of shopping.

Shop: Sharing knowledge and passion for food so they feel guided through the process.

Cook: Inspire trust in the freshness, whilst helping them cook their food at its very best and avoid food waste.

Working within a small blended team (UX, creative-tech and strategy) we created a number integrated solutions through social, web and data, workshopping with the client throughout the process.
