Honda Europe

An agile delivery and platform for the multi product divisions of Honda Europe.

Overview

I was responsible for leading the successful deployment of Honda Europe from concept to execution. The programme was agile consisting of 2 weeks sprints and the product was launched iteratively across 13 European markets. The team consisted of UI and content designers, UX, technology and two business owners from Honda, who based themselves in-house.
